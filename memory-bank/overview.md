# Overview del Progetto

We're launching an AI model aggregator that allows users to access multiple models with a single account and pay only for what they use. 
This eliminates the need for multiple subscriptions or relying on the same tool all the time. 
Our long-term vision is to recommend the best model based on the user's request and orchestrate multiple models and agents for complex tasks. 

We plan to include also other types of software in the future (e.g. e-mail marketing and lead research). 

Beyond the basic version, which includes free access to low-cost models and pay-per-use access to more expensive ones, we are designing Pro and Enterprise plans. 
The Pro plan offers a RAG system and other advanced features, while the Enterprise plan includes a management system that allows managers to monitor employees' AI usage, easily managing expenses and access. 

This solution integrates well with self-hosted open-source models, enhancing a company's sovereignty over its data. We will support both fiat and Bitcoin payments. Fiat users will load credit, which is then deducted based on usage, while Bitcoin users will be able to make microtransactions per single request without needing a deposit. 

In the future, we will integrate a non-custodial wallet, ensuring that users have full control over their funds, can withdraw them at any time, and can instantly distribute them among team members at no cost.
